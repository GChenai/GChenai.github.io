Hi, I am **GChen (顾志成)** from **Zhejiang University**.

My research focuses on **Smart Agriculture**, with interests in agricultural computer vision, crop phenotyping, multispectral image analysis, and lightweight recognition models for field-oriented applications.

芝士改变命运。

Research Interests
======

- Smart Agriculture
- Agricultural computer vision
- Tomato fruit detection and phenotyping
- Crop stress and drought recognition
- Multispectral image analysis
